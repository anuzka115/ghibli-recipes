INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tablespoon neutral-flavored oil, such as canola oil
  • 7 ounces thick-cut bacon or toucinho
  • 4 large eggs
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 to 4 teaspoons Jasmine tea leaves or any tea of your choice
  • loaf of ciabatta or any crusty bread of choice, sliced

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat the oil in a large cast-iron pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the bacon to the pan and fry until it is crispy on both slides, about 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Move the bacon to the sides of the pan. Crack the eggs directly into the same pan and cook until the egg whites are set, about 3 to 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In an electric kettle or a small saucepan, heat the water to a boil.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves and the boiling water to each bowl (use 2 to 4 bowls, depending how many people you are feeding). Let the tea steep for 3 minutes.
